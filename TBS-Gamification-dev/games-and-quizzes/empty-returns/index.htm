Instructions
1

Players have 60 seconds to accurately sort a variety of Empty Alcohol Containers that fall from the top of the screen (Beer bottles, wine and spirits, cans and other materials).

2

Players must “drag” the containers into the appropriate bins at the bottom of the screen to earn points. Each item that goes in the correct bin is 1 point.

3

As time goes on the speed and number of items dropping increases. The game concludes when the time runs out.
