How to Play
Instructions
1

Players have 60 seconds to accurately match a variety of Beer Types and Styles by flipping cards to find identical matches on the grid. (Stouts, Lagers, IPA, Ales, etc.).

2

Players will be given 4 seconds at the start of each level to memorize the grid before the timer resumes.

3

As players match and complete a grid they move to the next level and earn an equivalent amount of points per match as the level they are on. The higher the level, the more points the players earn per match. Every level a player completes, they earn an additional 10 points.

4

The game concludes when the timer runs out.
